Strategic notes from the GStack CEO specialist · live in your meeting

Positioning

  • WedgePick one painful job — note-taking, dev standups, sales recaps, customer success. Own one before going wide.
  • FrameBring your own AI teammate. Zoom owns the meeting; you own the brain that joins.
  • One-liner"Ship code with your AI team in the meeting." A verb, not a feature.
  • Demo90-second Loom showing one developer doing the work of five.

Three to nail before raising

  • 1DistributionHow does a dev hear about it Tuesday and have it in standup Wednesday?
  • 2PricingPer-seat or per-meeting? Tells investors which game you're playing.
  • 3EnemyReplacing the notetaker, the teammate, or the PM? Pick one. Hiding loses the round.

Find the enemy → find the budget

  • Note-takingOtter, Granola, Fireflies
  • AI meetingMicrosoft Copilot, Google Gemini-in-Meet
  • Voice agentVapi, Bland, Retell

Distribution playbook

  • 1Show HNTuesday morning Pacific. 90s Loom that proves the demo.
